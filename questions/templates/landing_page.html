Marc's study web-app for HTML5 certification

The HTML5 Professional Certification, also known as HTML5プロフェッショナル認定試験, is a certification designed to assess and validate one's skills in HTML5 and related web technologies. The certification is divided into two levels: Level 1 and Level 2. Level 1: Focus: This level covers the basics of HTML5, CSS3, and responsive web design. Exam Details: The exam consists of approximately 60 questions and takes about 90 minutes to complete. It is administered as a computer-based test (CBT), primarily involving multiple-choice and some short answer questions. Study Period: The typical study period is about 3 to 6 months. Requirements: There are no prerequisites for taking the Level 1 exam. Cost: ¥16,500 (tax included) I built this with Django. 📝Project notes.
